## Study method (remediation loop)

| Concept | Type | Gloss |
|---|---|---|
| Deconstruct practice questions | concept | Mark every uncertain answer; review all wrong/uncertain before the next set. |
| Restudy before retesting | concept | Only take another practice set after you can explain the missed concept. |
| Answer what is asked | exam_trap | Pick the BEST/FIRST action the stem asks for, not merely a true statement. |
| Time management | concept | Practice full-length under time; move on rather than fixate on one item. |
| Wrong-answer journal | concept | Record the missed keyword, the concept to restudy, and a one-sentence rule. |

## Learning-science anchors

| Concept | Type | Gloss |
|---|---|---|
| Retrieval practice | concept | Active recall (testing yourself) outperforms passive rereading. |
| Distributed practice | concept | Spacing study over time beats massed cramming for retention. |
